Intellitax Questionnaire

1. Income and Employment:

What is your total annual income, including salary, bonuses, and any additional sources of income?

Are you a salaried employee, self-employed, or do you have income from investments?

2. Deductions and Credits:

Do you have any eligible deductions, such as mortgage interest, student loan interest, or contributions to retirement accounts?

Are you eligible for any tax credits, such as child tax credits or education credits?

3. Investments and Capital Gains:

Have you earned any income from investments, dividends, or capital gains this fiscal year?

Are you aware of the tax implications of your investment portfolio?

4. Expenses and Debt Indicators:

What are your monthly living expenses, including rent or mortgage, utilities, and other recurring costs?

Do you have any outstanding loans or credit card debt? If yes, what is the approximate amount?

5. Financial Goals and Risk Tolerance:

What are your short-term and long-term financial goals?

How would you describe your risk tolerance when it comes to financial investments?

6. Future Financial Planning:

Are you planning any significant financial changes in the upcoming year, such as buying a home, starting a business, or pursuing higher education?

7. Tax Deduction Tips:

Based on your profile, Intellitax may suggest personalized tax deduction strategies, such as maximizing contributions to tax-advantaged accounts or taking advantage of specific deductions based on your circumstances.

8. Debt Risk Assessment:

Analyzing the user's debt-to-income ratio and credit card utilization to assess potential debt issues.

Asking about the user's emergency fund and financial safety nets.

9. Tax Liability Projection:

Using the gathered information to project the user's total tax liability at the end of the fiscal year.

10. Actionable Insights:

Providing actionable insights on how the user can optimize their finances to minimize tax liability and reduce the risk of debt.
